Every individual is created by God with the rights and privileges to reach their full potential with dignity and respect and to make a positive impact on their family, community and country.

GIFT is dedicated to nurturing the well-being of all individuals, focusing on those less fortunate, to foster growth in body, mind, soul, and spirit. We aim to uplift families and build a stronger community.

  • Health: Offering medical and dental care, eye exams, health education, and improved access to clean water and sanitation.
  • Organic Farming: Educating on organic practices, establishing a model farm, and providing micro-financing for farmers.
  • Church: Creating a local church for spiritual growth and community support.
  • Education: Providing sponsorships, supplies for children, adult education, and support for local teachers.
